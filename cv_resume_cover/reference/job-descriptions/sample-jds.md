# Sample Job Descriptions for EIC Career Targeting

> **Purpose:** Six summarized job descriptions across sectors relevant to EIC users from diverse majors. Each includes tagged ATS keywords to help users align their resumes with real-world postings.
>
> **How to use:** When building or tailoring a resume, scan the keywords section for the role you're targeting. Mirror these terms in your bullet points and skills section. Pair with the [Translation Guide](../translation-guide.md) to convert academic experience into industry language.

---

## 1. Research Coordinator — Health Tech Startup

**Sector:** Healthcare Technology / Digital Health
**Level:** Entry-level to early career (0–2 years)
**Best-fit majors:** Psychology, Biology, Public Health, Neuroscience

### Core Responsibilities

- Coordinate **IRB submissions** and ensure **regulatory compliance** for clinical and user-experience studies
- Recruit, screen, and schedule **research participants** across multiple concurrent studies
- Maintain **research databases** and ensure **data integrity** using REDCap or similar platforms
- Assist principal investigators with **literature reviews**, protocol development, and **study design**
- Prepare **progress reports**, **data summaries**, and internal presentations for cross-functional teams
- Manage vendor relationships and track **project timelines** using project management tools
- Support **grant applications** and institutional reporting requirements

### Required Skills / Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Psychology, Biology, Public Health, or related field
- Familiarity with **research methodologies** (quantitative and qualitative)
- Experience with **IRB protocols** and **human subjects research**
- Proficiency in **Microsoft Office Suite** and **data management tools** (REDCap, SPSS, or Excel)
- Strong **written and verbal communication** skills
- Detail-oriented with ability to manage **multiple priorities**

### ATS Keywords

> **research coordination**, **IRB**, **regulatory compliance**, **data management**, **REDCap**, **participant recruitment**, **study design**, **literature review**, **clinical research**, **project management**, **SPSS**, **data integrity**, **progress reports**, **cross-functional**, **human subjects research**, **protocol development**

## 2. Junior Content Strategist — B2B SaaS Company

**Sector:** Software-as-a-Service / Technology
**Level:** Entry-level (0–1 year or internship-to-hire)
**Best-fit majors:** Philosophy, Marketing, Communications, English, Journalism

### Core Responsibilities

- Develop and maintain a **content calendar** aligned with product launches and **go-to-market strategy**
- Write and edit **blog posts**, **case studies**, **white papers**, and **email campaigns**
- Conduct **audience research** and **competitive analysis** to identify content gaps and opportunities
- Collaborate with product marketing, design, and sales teams on **messaging frameworks**
- Optimize content for **SEO** using keyword research tools (Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Google Search Console)
- Track **content performance metrics** (engagement, conversion, traffic) and report insights to stakeholders
- Manage **CMS platforms** (WordPress, HubSpot, or Contentful) for publishing workflows

### Required Skills / Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, English, Philosophy, or related field
- Strong **analytical writing** and **editing** skills with portfolio samples
- Understanding of **B2B buyer journey** and **content marketing** fundamentals
- Familiarity with **SEO best practices** and at least one **analytics platform** (Google Analytics)
- Ability to translate complex or technical topics into clear, compelling narratives
- Experience with **social media management** and **email marketing platforms** is a plus

### ATS Keywords

> **content strategy**, **B2B**, **SaaS**, **content calendar**, **SEO**, **keyword research**, **content marketing**, **blog posts**, **case studies**, **white papers**, **CMS**, **Google Analytics**, **email campaigns**, **audience research**, **competitive analysis**, **go-to-market**, **messaging**, **editorial**, **conversion**, **engagement metrics**

## 3. Data Analyst — Entry-Level, Management Consulting

**Sector:** Consulting / Professional Services
**Level:** Entry-level (0–2 years; analyst program)
**Best-fit majors:** Mathematics, Economics, Engineering, Statistics, Computer Science

### Core Responsibilities

- Collect, clean, and analyze **large datasets** to support client engagements and internal strategy
- Build **dashboards** and **data visualizations** using **Tableau**, **Power BI**, or similar tools
- Perform **statistical analysis** and **trend modeling** to generate actionable insights
- Prepare **client-ready deliverables** including slide decks, memos, and executive summaries
- Support senior consultants with **market research**, **benchmarking**, and **financial modeling**
- Write and maintain **SQL queries** to extract and transform data from relational databases
- Communicate findings to both technical and non-technical **stakeholders**

### Required Skills / Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Mathematics, Economics, Statistics, Engineering, or quantitative field
- Proficiency in **SQL**, **Excel** (advanced: pivot tables, VLOOKUP, macros), and at least one **visualization tool**
- Experience with **Python** or **R** for data analysis is preferred
- Strong **problem-solving** and **critical thinking** skills
- Ability to work in **fast-paced, client-facing** environments
- Excellent **presentation skills** and attention to detail

### ATS Keywords

> **data analysis**, **SQL**, **Python**, **R**, **Tableau**, **Power BI**, **Excel**, **statistical analysis**, **data visualization**, **dashboards**, **financial modeling**, **market research**, **benchmarking**, **consulting**, **client-facing**, **problem-solving**, **quantitative analysis**, **trend analysis**, **ETL**, **stakeholder communication**

## 4. Program Coordinator — Nonprofit / Community Development

**Sector:** Nonprofit / Social Services / Community Development
**Level:** Entry-level to early career (0–2 years)
**Best-fit majors:** Anthropology, Sociology, Social Work, Political Science, Public Policy

### Core Responsibilities

- Plan, implement, and evaluate **community programs** and **outreach initiatives**
- Build and maintain relationships with **community partners**, local organizations, and **government agencies**
- Collect and analyze **program data** to measure outcomes, track **KPIs**, and prepare **impact reports**
- Coordinate **volunteer recruitment**, onboarding, and scheduling across multiple sites
- Assist with **grant writing**, **fundraising events**, and donor stewardship activities
- Facilitate **workshops**, **focus groups**, and community listening sessions
- Manage **program budgets** and ensure compliance with **funder requirements**

### Required Skills / Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Sociology, Anthropology, Social Work, Public Policy, or related field
- Experience with **community engagement** or **program management** (internships and campus roles count)
- Familiarity with **data collection** methods and basic **program evaluation**
- Strong **interpersonal skills** and cultural competency when working with **diverse populations**
- Proficiency in **Microsoft Office** and **database management** (Salesforce NPSP, CiviCRM, or similar)
- Ability to manage **multiple projects** and meet deadlines in a dynamic environment

### ATS Keywords

> **program coordination**, **community engagement**, **outreach**, **grant writing**, **fundraising**, **volunteer management**, **program evaluation**, **KPIs**, **impact reporting**, **stakeholder relations**, **cultural competency**, **diverse populations**, **budget management**, **nonprofit**, **community partnerships**, **data collection**, **workshop facilitation**, **case management**

## 5. Marketing Associate — Consumer Brand

**Sector:** Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) / Retail / Direct-to-Consumer
**Level:** Entry-level (0–1 year)
**Best-fit majors:** Marketing, Communications, Business, Graphic Design, Psychology

### Core Responsibilities

- Support execution of **integrated marketing campaigns** across digital and traditional channels
- Manage **social media accounts** (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn) including content creation, scheduling, and **community management**
- Assist with **email marketing** campaigns using platforms like Mailchimp, Klaviyo, or HubSpot
- Coordinate with external **agencies** and **influencers** on brand partnerships and sponsored content
- Monitor **campaign performance** using **Google Analytics**, social insights, and A/B testing results
- Conduct **consumer research** and **trend analysis** to inform brand positioning and creative briefs
- Maintain **brand guidelines** and ensure visual and tonal consistency across all touchpoints

### Required Skills / Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field
- Understanding of **digital marketing channels** (social, email, paid media, SEO/SEM)
- Experience with **social media management tools** (Sprout Social, Hootsuite, Later)
- Basic proficiency in **Adobe Creative Suite** or **Canva** for design assets
- Strong **copywriting** skills and eye for **brand voice**
- Analytical mindset with ability to interpret **marketing metrics** and translate data into recommendations

### ATS Keywords

> **marketing campaigns**, **social media management**, **content creation**, **email marketing**, **Google Analytics**, **brand management**, **digital marketing**, **influencer marketing**, **community management**, **consumer research**, **A/B testing**, **campaign performance**, **brand guidelines**, **copywriting**, **trend analysis**, **paid media**, **SEO**, **SEM**, **CPG**, **creative briefs**

## 6. Teaching Assistant / Academic Support Specialist — University

**Sector:** Higher Education / Academic Services
**Level:** Entry-level or graduate (any experience level)
**Best-fit majors:** Any major (position values subject expertise + communication skills)

### Core Responsibilities

- Lead **discussion sections**, **review sessions**, and **office hours** for undergraduate courses
- Develop and deliver **instructional materials**, study guides, and supplemental learning resources
- Grade **assignments**, **exams**, and written work using established rubrics and providing **constructive feedback**
- Support students through **one-on-one tutoring**, **academic coaching**, and **mentoring**
- Track **student progress** and flag at-risk students to faculty for early intervention
- Collaborate with faculty on **curriculum development**, lesson planning, and **assessment design**
- Maintain **accurate records** in **learning management systems** (Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle)

### Required Skills / Qualifications

- Enrollment in or completion of a bachelor's or master's degree program
- Strong **subject matter expertise** in relevant discipline
- Excellent **verbal communication** and **public speaking** skills
- Demonstrated ability to explain complex concepts to **diverse learners**
- Patience, empathy, and commitment to **inclusive pedagogy**
- Familiarity with **educational technology** tools and **LMS platforms**
- Time management skills to balance teaching responsibilities with personal coursework

### ATS Keywords

> **teaching assistant**, **academic support**, **tutoring**, **curriculum development**, **instructional design**, **assessment**, **grading**, **rubrics**, **learning management system**, **Canvas**, **Blackboard**, **mentoring**, **student success**, **office hours**, **inclusive pedagogy**, **feedback**, **lesson planning**, **educational technology**, **discussion facilitation**, **academic coaching**
