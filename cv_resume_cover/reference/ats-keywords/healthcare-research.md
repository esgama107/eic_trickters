# ATS Keyword Glossary — Healthcare & Research

> **What this is:** A reference list of high-frequency keywords that Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) scan for in healthcare and research-sector resumes. These terms appear consistently across job postings for early-in-career roles. Weave relevant terms into your resume's skills section, bullet points, and summary to improve your match rate.

---
