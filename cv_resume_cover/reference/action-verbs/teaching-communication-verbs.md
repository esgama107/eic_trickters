# Action Verbs: Teaching & Communication

Clear communication is central to nearly every early-career role—whether you tutored peers, wrote for a campus publication, or presented research at a symposium. This reference groups verbs by **communication context** so you can match the right verb to what you actually did.

> **How to use this list:** Identify the type of communication work you performed, select a precise verb, and pair it with specifics—*who* you communicated with, *what* you conveyed, and *the scope or impact*.

---
